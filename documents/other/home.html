About Achievements

Avi Sharma

The child prodigy from Indore, Avi Sharma with young age of 12, World book of record & India Book of Record Holder Avi is child having IQ 161, more than Albert Einstein & famous as *wonder boy* he is an Author, Motivational Speaker, Youngest Mentor of Vedic maths, Actor & Young coder. As Child artist Avi Sharma performed in many TV shows on several channels & TV commercials. Avi utilized his lockdown (2020) to rewrite Ramayana in Hindi in short and melodious way named BalMukhi Ramayana in 250 Chands for to spread moral values like Respect, Patience, obedience, bravery, honesty. During Lockdown 2021 Avi taught vedic mathematics to more than 150 children from Classes 5 to 12 for free of cost during pandemic with the aim of helping students overcome maths anxiety. Recently Avi has created Alexa like voice assistant Named “MADHAV” to help old and disabled people, in python language of software which he learns from his own. Most importantly He's also teaching coding to many students for free. Avi wants to contribute in digital India mission of our Respected P.M. Modiji. He has also delivered lectures on motivation and management in various college & universities. He is working to inculcate moral values, leadership skills, and motivation among people, especially children Under "Mission Shiksha & Sanskar". Avi Sharma also runs a celebrity talk show online called "Celebrity Corner" in which has interviewed more than 70 people who are doing extraordinary work. Avi won 2 India Book of Records and the World Book of Records, Youth Icon Award 2021, Exclusive World Records, the Pride of India award, omg book of record, National excellence award, 2 international icon award & honour from Indo UK cultural forum. He has bagged 20 gold and 1 silver medal in different Olympiads till date. Hobbies of Avi is to read and write books & mentoring younger generation.
